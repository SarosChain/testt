SAROS METARACE GAMES

SAROS is an NFT-based game dog racing game. SAROS was built on the blockchain to simulate real-life racing experiences. It brings dogs racing into a futuristic virtual reality. The play-to-earn game follows the trend of new generation Crypto coins by ensuring it has a well-defined use case and utility. The perfect combination of NFTs and P2E Bread - Race - Trade - Earn. Create a legacy by building a star-studded stable of winning racedogs.

SWAP

SAROS Swap is the official decentralized exchange (DEX) of our community that works like a bank entity on our platform. It gives holders the ability to swap independently to buy SAROS tokens directly and also convert their winning rewards to another currency. Furthermore, it acts as the foundation upon which many of SAROS's ongoing community development efforts will integrated with the marketplace, games, which makes the platform’s overall in-game app experience smoother, faster, and hassle-free.
